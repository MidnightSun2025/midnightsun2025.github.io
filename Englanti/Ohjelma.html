Mensa Midnight Sun 2025

You are more than welcome to join us on our 4th Mensa Midnight Sun happening on Finnish lapland wildernes. You will experience some Lappish traditions, meet new (and old) faces and much more. Not to mention midnight sun itself!

The price of the meeting is 334 €/person and the price includes accommodation, breakfasts, lunches, dinners, transportation from/to Rovaniemi from our action center and all the programs as well as great bunch of people. (No alcoholic drinks are included so BYOB.)

The maximum number of participants is 50 person and minimum half of that are reserved to foreigners. Please bear in mind also that the meeting is intended only for the members of Mensa. The last registration day is 31st of March and the last day to pay the admission is 30th of April. Places will be filled in the registration order.
